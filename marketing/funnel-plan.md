# Laws of AI Agents: monetization funnel plan

The goal: turn free traffic into email subscribers, warm them with a 5-lesson course, and
convert a slice into buyers of the expanded PDF. Keep it cheap and simple to run.

## The funnel in one picture

1. Visitor lands on laws.deleg8.dev (free, already live, now tracked in GA4).
2. On-site signup block offers the free 5-lesson email course in exchange for an email.
3. The course delivers one law every 5 days. Each email gives real value and points back to the site.
4. Email 5 makes the offer: the expanded 48-page PDF, all 50 laws with examples and sources.
5. Checkout is PayPal (and card). On payment, the buyer is auto-sent the PDF and a receipt.

Everything except step 5 is free to run. Step 5 only costs PayPal's per-transaction fee.

## What to use: one tool does almost all of it

The easiest path is a single creator email platform that handles the list, the course
automation, and selling the PDF with PayPal and automatic delivery. You do not need a backend,
a database, or a Cloud Run function.

### Recommended: Kit (formerly ConvertKit)
- Free up to 10,000 subscribers.
- Signup forms you embed on the site (gives you a form POST URL and an email field name).
- Sequences for the 5-lesson course (set a 5-day delay between steps).
- Kit Commerce sells the PDF as a digital product, takes PayPal and Stripe, and auto-delivers
  the file plus a receipt, then tags the buyer as a customer.
- One dashboard, one login, no glue code.

### Alternative: MailerLite
- Free up to 1,000 subscribers (lower cap, but very clean templates).
- Same capabilities: embeddable forms, automations for the course, sell digital products with
  PayPal and Stripe, automatic file delivery.
- Pick this if you prefer its editor or are fine with the 1,000 cap to start.

Both connect your PayPal in their commerce settings. Buyer pays with PayPal, the tool emails
the PDF and receipt, no manual work.

### If you insist on PayPal only, no email tool
- Use a PayPal Payment Link or Buy Now button. On payment, PayPal can deliver a digital good or
  redirect to a thank-you page that hosts the PDF.
- Downside: you do not capture the email into a list and you cannot run the course, so you lose
  the whole top of the funnel. Not recommended. The email tool route is both easier and better.

## Pricing
- Suggested launch price for the PDF: 19 USD. Round, low-friction, expensable.
- Optional: a name-your-price floor of 9 USD if you want volume over margin early.
- Keep the site and the 5-lesson course free forever. The free tier is what makes it spread.

## What is already built in this repo
- The expanded PDF: `pdf/Laws-of-AI-Agents.pdf` (48 pages), generated by `build-pdf.mjs` from
  `data/laws.json` plus `data/examples.json`. Rebuild after editing laws with:
  `node build-pdf.mjs` then render the HTML in `pdf/` to PDF with headless Chrome.
- The 5-lesson email course copy: `marketing/email-course.md`. Paste each email as a sequence step.
- The on-site signup block: built into the site, currently OFF. It renders only when you set
  `site.newsletter.action` in `data/laws.json`.

## To go live (your decisions, then a 5-minute wiring step)
1. Pick the email tool (Kit recommended).
2. Create the account, connect PayPal in its commerce settings.
3. Create a signup form. Copy its POST action URL and the email field name.
4. In `data/laws.json`, set:
   - `site.newsletter.action` to the form POST URL
   - `site.newsletter.field` to the email field name (Kit uses `email_address`, MailerLite often `fields[email]` or `email`)
   Rebuild and deploy. The signup block appears on the site and starts collecting emails.
5. Create the 5-step sequence from `marketing/email-course.md`, 5-day delays.
6. Create the digital product for `pdf/Laws-of-AI-Agents.pdf`, price it, enable PayPal.
7. Put the product link into Email 5 where it says `[PDF LINK]`.

That is the whole funnel. After this it runs itself.
