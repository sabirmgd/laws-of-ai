# Laws of AI Agents: monetization funnel plan

The goal: turn free traffic into email subscribers, warm them with a 5-lesson course, and
convert a slice into buyers of the AI Agent Audit Kit. Keep it cheap and simple to run.

## The funnel in one picture

1. Visitor lands on laws.deleg8.dev (free, already live, now tracked in GA4).
2. On-site signup block offers the free 5-lesson email course in exchange for an email.
3. The course delivers one law every 5 days. Each email gives real value and points back to the site.
4. Email 5 makes the offer: the AI Agent Audit Kit, an installable audit skill plus protected access to the expanded online 50-law digital edition.
5. Checkout is PayPal/card through the on-site PayPal button. On capture, the server grants access and the buyer can unlock `/access` with the checkout email.

Everything except checkout and the small Cloud Run/GCP footprint is cheap to run. The goal is fast validation, not a complex platform.

## What to use: one tool does almost all of it

The split is now: Kit handles the free newsletter and 5-email course; PayPal handles payment; Cloud Run creates/captures orders and serves paid access; Firestore stores buyer entitlements.

The operational runbook lives in `marketing/launch-runbook.md`. Use `marketing/offer.env.example` plus `node scripts/apply-offer-config.mjs` to apply public offer and newsletter values.

### Newsletter: Kit (formerly ConvertKit)
- Free up to 10,000 subscribers.
- Signup forms you embed on the site (gives you a form POST URL and an email field name).
- One sequence is enough for the 5-lesson course (set a 5-day delay between steps).
- Use this for email only. Do not pay for a newsletter tool until the offer validates.

### Payment and access: PayPal + Firestore
- Use a PayPal REST app under the existing merchant account.
- Product: `AI Agent Audit Kit: 50 Laws Edition`.
- Price: 14.90 USD.
- Configure PayPal webhook URL: `https://laws.deleg8.dev/api/paypal/webhook`.
- The product page uses `/api/paypal/create-order` and `/api/paypal/capture-order`.

Buyer pays with PayPal/card, PayPal sends the receipt, and the site unlocks the protected edition for the buyer email.

## Pricing
- Launch price for the kit: 14.90 USD.
- Keep the site and the 5-lesson course free forever. The free tier is what makes it spread.

## What is already built in this repo
- The protected illustrated online digital edition: `dist/edition.html`, generated by `build.mjs` from
  `data/laws.json`, `data/examples.json`, and the image assets.
- The paid kit source: `product/ai-agent-audit-kit/`.
- The installable `ai-agent-audit` skill, generated 50-law rubric, templates, prompts, and sample audit.
- The paid access server: `server.mjs`, including `/api/paypal/create-order`, `/api/paypal/capture-order`, `/api/paypal/webhook`, `/access`, and `/paid/...`.
- The 5-lesson email course copy: `marketing/email-course.md`. Paste each email as a sequence step.
- The on-site signup block posts to `/api/newsletter` when Kit credentials are configured.

## To go live (your decisions, then a 5-minute wiring step)
1. Create the Kit account for email.
2. Use the existing Creative PayPal REST app credentials, or create a separate Laws PayPal REST app later.
3. Create a Kit signup form or use the server-side Kit V4 key already configured locally.
4. In `.env.local`, set:
   - `PAYPAL_CLIENT_ID`
   - `PAYPAL_CLIENT_SECRET`
   - `PAYPAL_MODE`
   - `PAYPAL_WEBHOOK_ID` if already created
   - `KIT_FORM_ID`
   - `KIT_V4_API_KEY`
5. Create the 5-step sequence from `marketing/email-course.md`, 5-day delays.
6. Let `scripts/deploy.sh` create or reuse the PayPal webhook for `https://laws.deleg8.dev/api/paypal/webhook`.
7. Deploy with `bash scripts/deploy.sh`; it creates/checks Firestore, uploads the protected edition to GCS, and mounts secrets.
8. Put the product link into Email 5 where it says `[KIT LINK]`.
9. Run a sandbox PayPal checkout first, then switch `PAYPAL_MODE=live` and run a real low-risk purchase/refund test.

That is the whole funnel. After this it runs itself.
